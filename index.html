ProjectSin is my brainchild of a concept global brand. Ever since I got immersed in the digital domain, the thought of having my own brand as an individual also emerged. And so, in the mid months of 2019, ProjectSin was born. At first I struggled whether to name it ProjectSin or ProjectsIn (since it would give a subcontextual ring to it), but alas I kept my original plan. It would be the flagship brand for all the upcoming projects, and ventures. At first the concept was to furnish and to introduce myself as a digital artist. I tried to create my own style and shifting from traditional art to digital. As of today, it is still at its hatchling form. Manifesting that someday it would become one of the many known recognized brands worldwide.
